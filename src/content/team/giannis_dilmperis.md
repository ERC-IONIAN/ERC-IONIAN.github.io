Giannis Dilmperis is a **PhD Candidate** in the IONIAN group at ETRO, Vrije Universiteit Brussel (VUB), supervised by [Nikos Deligiannis](/team/nikos_deligiannis/). He is an AI researcher with a background in mathematics and machine learning, with interests in computer vision, data attribution and physics-informed neural networks. His work combines theoretical depth with practical model development, including experience with transformers, action recognition, and neural methods for solving differential equations. He holds an MSc in Artificial Intelligence from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki and previously conducted research at CERTH, where he developed deep learning models for human activity recognition.
